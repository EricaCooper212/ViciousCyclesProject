Welcome to Vicious Cycles, a serious bike store for a serious pastime.

We offer top-quality cycling equipment, the supply line of choice. We are a name you can trust at a price you can afford.

The Vicious Cycle community is a group of individuals who care about you as a cyclist, providing the equipment you need. Not only do we provide the gear and service you need, you can also join one of our groups which provide an environment where you can learn and grow no matter your level of biking. Connecting with friends and nature might be the best part of your week. Our favorite read? A heartwarming story of two friends cycling on the Great Allegheny Passage. This reinforces our belief in the friendship that can develop through cycling adventures. Get your copy here.

We are a name you can trust. Led by local enthusiasts, Vicious Cycles is a cycle shop with a reputation in innovative custom designs. Choose from our in-store brands or make an appointment for your in-house customization.

Our store is unique because we want you to become a better rider, no matter the level - from beginner to competitor. We believe that the bick should be an extension of the owner.

We bike hard. We bike often. Get up, fall, get up and ride again. With Vicious Cycles.
