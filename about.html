The Vicious Cycles team is a group of passionate cyclists who are ready to share our passion with you. From helping you choose a new bike to setting up your leveled biking group, we want to walk with you in your journey as a cyclist. From gear to community, we are here for you.

Owen Hayes

Owen is a passionate cyclist who has been on wheels since he was young. He He is the owner of Vicious Cycles, and the founder of the Raleigh Cycling Club. He has a passion for building the perfect setup for each customer. His favorite vacations are taking bicycle tours through Europe. He loves the community of friends he has made. He wants to continue to build the NC community of cyclist through events and rides at Vicious Cycles. When not at the shop, Owen is playing with his two dogs, Max and Roxie.

Max Anderson

Max is a fix-it kind of guy, who is always tinkering with something around the house. He combined his love of engineering with cycling, and after working many years for the Trek company has decided to come back to NC and work in our shop! He loves riding the trails around town and in the mountains. If you are interested in bikepacking, Max is your go-to man!

Melanie Ashford

Melanie started riding after college, when she met her husband Jarrad. She finds great routes for weekend rides through the back roads of NC. During the week, Melanie is our customer service specialist, who will respond to your emails, answer your phone call, or greet you when you come into the store.
